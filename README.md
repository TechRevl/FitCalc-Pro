Your Privacy Matters
FitCalc Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe your health and fitness data should remain private and under your control. This mobile app is designed with privacy-first principles.

Data Collection & Storage
We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal health data to external servers.

All calculations are performed locally on your Android device
No health data sent to servers: Your weight, height, body fat %, and other metrics stay on your device
Local storage only: Calculation history, coins, and preferences stored in device storage (localStorage)
No user accounts required: No email, phone number, or personal information collected
No analytics tracking: We don't track which calculators you use or how often
What Data Stays on Your Device
The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

Calculation History: Your past calculations and results (up to 100 entries)
Coin Balance: Your current coin count and purchase receipts
App Preferences: Dark mode setting and startup ad preference
Purchase Receipts: Google Play transaction IDs for restore purchases feature
Third-Party Services
FitCalc Pro integrates with the following third-party services for app functionality:

Google AdMob: Displays banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data per Google's privacy policy.
Google Play Billing: Processes in-app purchases securely. Transaction data is handled by Google Play per their privacy policy.
No other third parties: We don't use analytics platforms, crash reporting, or user tracking services.
For more information on how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Android Permissions
FitCalc Pro requests the following Android permissions:

INTERNET: Required for displaying ads and processing in-app purchases
BILLING: Required for Google Play in-app purchases
No sensitive permissions: We don't access camera, microphone, contacts, location, or files
Data Deletion
You have full control over your data:

Clear History: Delete calculation history anytime from the History page
Uninstall App: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
Clear App Data: Use Android Settings → Apps → FitCalc Pro → Clear Data
In-App Purchases & Refunds
Coin purchases are processed securely through Google Play:

Secure Payments: All transactions handled by Google Play Billing
Receipt Storage: Purchase receipts stored locally for restore functionality
Restore Purchases: Use "Restore Purchases" button to recover coins on new devices
Refunds: Managed through Google Play Store per their refund policy
Medical Disclaimer
Important: FitCalc Pro provides fitness and health calculations for informational purposes only. These calculators are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals before making decisions based on these calculations, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions or concerns.

Accuracy & Limitations
While we use scientifically validated formulas and regularly update our calculators to reflect the latest research, all calculations provide estimates based on population averages. Individual results may vary based on genetics, health conditions, medications, and other factors. For personalized advice, consult with healthcare or fitness professionals.

Children's Privacy
FitCalc Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to Privacy Policy
If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date below. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. However, our core commitment to not collecting personal health data will remain unchanged.

Contact Us
If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing or email us at support@fitcalcpro.com.
